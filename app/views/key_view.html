What are the table headers?

The table headers are part of the Field Register. This is what they stand for:

School-eng:
School in England legally registered to provide full time education to pupils of compulsory school age.
Name:
Name of field
Address:
An address in the UK.
School-authority-eng:
School authority organisation.
Minimum-age:
Minimum age.
Maximum-age:
Maximum age.
School-capacity:
A school’s capacity of pupil places.
Religious-characters:
A list of religion or subgroup within a religion operating under a common name, tradition, and identity.
Religious-ethos:
A list of religion or subgroup within a religion operating under a common name, tradition, and identity.
Dioceses:
A list of districts under the pastoral care of a bishop in the Christian Church.
Organisation:
A legal entity.
School-phases:
A list of phases of a school.
School-admissions-policy:
The admissions policy for a school.
School-gender:
The gender of a school.
School-tags:
Set of tags which may be applied to a school.
Headteacher:
The name of the headteacher or principal for a school or educational establishment.
Start-date:
Start datetime for the applicability of a register entry.
End-date:
End datetime for the applicability of a register entry.
